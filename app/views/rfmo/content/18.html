Contracting parties: 25 total: Australia, Canada, China, Cook Islands, EU, Federated States of Micronesia, Fiji, France, Japan, Kiribati, Korea, Marshall Islands (Republic of), Nauru, New Zealand, Niue, Palau, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Taiwan, Tonga, Tuvalu, USA, Vanuatu.

Area: Western and Central Pacific Ocean.

Date entered into force: 2004

FAO association: No.

FAO statistical area: 81, 71, 61, 67, 77.

Objectives: “…to ensure, through effective management, the long-term conservation and sustainable use of highly migratory fish stocks in the western and central Pacific Ocean in accordance with the 1982 United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea and the 1995 UN Fish Stocks Agreement.”

Primary species: All species of highly migratory fish stocks within the Convention Area, except sauries.
