Area: The Indian Ocean and adjacent seas, north of the Antarctic Convergence.

Date entered into force: 1996

Objectives: “…to promote cooperation among its Members with a view to ensuring, through appropriate management, the conservation and optimum utilization of stocks and encouraging sustainable development of fisheries based on such stocks.”
