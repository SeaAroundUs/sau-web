Contracting parties: 12 total: Canada, Cuba, Denmark (Faroe Islands & Greenland), EU, France (Saint Pierre et Miquelon), Iceland, Japan, Korea (Republic of), Norway, Russian Federation, Ukraine, USA.

Area: Northwest Atlantic.

Date entered into force: 1979

FAO association: No.

FAO statistical area: 21.

Objectives: “…to contribute through consultation and cooperation to the optimum utilization, rational management and conservation of the fishery resources of the Convention Area.”

Primary species: American plaice (Hippoglossoides platessoides), Atlantic cod (Gadus morhua), Atlantic redfishes (Sebastes spp.), Capelin (Mallotus villosus), Greenland halibut (Reinhardtius hippoglossoides), Northern shrimp (Pandalus spp.), Shortfinned squid (Illex illecebrosus), Thorny skate (Amblyraja radiata), White hake (Urophycis tenuis), Witch flounder (Glyptocephalus cynoglossus), Yellowtail flounder (Limanda ferruginea).
